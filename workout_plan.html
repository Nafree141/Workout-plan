Principles behind this plan

  • Upper/Lower split — trains each muscle group twice per week, optimal for beginner hypertrophy
  • Progressive overload — add weight only when you hit the top of your rep range with clean form
  • Caloric surplus of ~300 kcal — lean bulk to minimise fat gain (TDEE ~2,750 kcal)
  • Form before weight — prioritise learning the movement pattern first
  • Consistency beats perfection — 80% adherence over 12 weeks beats the perfect plan followed for 3
Starting June: spend the first 2 weeks as technique weeks with lighter weights. Begin full progression from week 3.

Form cues — Monday

▾
Dumbbell Bench Press: Lie flat, retract scapulae. DBs at chest, palms forward. Press up and slightly inward so DBs nearly touch at top. Lower slowly 2–3s — use the extra range of motion vs barbell. Elbows at ~75°. Don't let DBs drift too wide at top.

Seated Cable Row: Sit tall, chest up. Pull elbows straight back, squeeze shoulder blades at end. Avoid rounding forward. Control the return.

DB Overhead Press: Sit on 90° bench, DBs at shoulder height. Press straight up. No excessive lower back arch. Lower slowly.

Lat Pulldown: Grip just outside shoulder-width. Slight lean back. Pull bar to upper chest, lead with elbows. Squeeze lats at bottom. Never pull behind the neck.

Incline DB Fly: Bench at 30–45°. Slight elbow bend throughout. Wide arc down until deep chest stretch. Drive elbows back up — don't turn it into a press.

Tricep Pushdown: Elbows tucked and fixed at sides. Push until full extension. Squeeze hard at bottom. Controlled return.

Form cues — Tuesday

▾
Back Squat: Bar on traps, feet shoulder-width, toes 10–30° out. Brace core hard. Push hips back and bend knees together. Chest up, back flat. Thighs to parallel or below. Drive through heels. Knees track over toes — no valgus collapse inward.

Romanian Deadlift: Hinge at hips, push them straight back. Back completely flat. Lower bar along legs until deep hamstring stretch. Drive hips forward to stand.

Leg Press: Feet hip-width, mid-platform. Whole foot contact. Knees to ~90°. Don't fully lock out at top. Lower back into the pad throughout.

Lying Leg Curl: Curl heels to glutes. Pause and squeeze at top. Lower slowly. Don't let hips lift.

Calf Raise: Full range — all the way up, all the way down until you feel the stretch. Pause 1 second at the bottom stretch.

Form cues — Thursday

▾
OHP: Bar on front delts. Brace core, squeeze glutes to prevent lower back arch. Press straight overhead and lock out. Lower slowly.

Assisted Pull-up: Grip slightly wider than shoulder-width. Pull elbows down to hips. Chin clears bar. Lower over 2–3 seconds. No kipping or swinging.

Incline DB Bench Press: Bench at 30–45°. Retract scapulae. Press up and slightly inward. Elbows ~75°. Don't go above 45° — too much front delt involvement.

Single-Arm DB Row: Knee and hand on bench for support. Back flat. Pull DB toward hip, not chest. Squeeze lat at top. Don't rotate torso to cheat the weight up.

Curl: Elbows fixed at sides — they must not move. Full range. No swinging.

Lateral Raises: Lead with elbows. Raise to shoulder height only — no higher. 2-second controlled descent. Go lighter than your ego wants.

Form cues — Friday

▾
Conventional Deadlift: Bar over mid-foot. Back flat, chest up. Drive floor away — don't think of it as pulling. Bar stays against legs the whole way. Lock out with hips extended and glutes squeezed. Build tension before lifting — no jerking off the floor.

Goblet Squat: DB at chest. Squat deep. Great for grooving squat mechanics before barbell work.

Hip Thrust: Upper back on bench edge, bar across hips with a pad. Drive hips up until torso is parallel to floor. Squeeze glutes hard — hold 1 second. Don't hyperextend your lower back at the top.

Leg Extension: Pad just above ankle. Extend fully, squeeze quads hard at top. Lower with control. Keep hips in the seat.

Saturday cheat day

  • One junk meal will not undo a week of progress
  • Keep other Saturday meals close to normal
  • Still try to hit ~170g protein across the day
  • Weekly calorie averages matter more than any single day

Key nutrition notes

  • Hydration: 3–4 litres per day, more on training days
  • Creatine monohydrate: 5g daily — the most evidence-backed supplement for muscle gain
  • Adjust after 4 weeks: weight not rising? Add 100–200 kcal. Gaining fat fast? Cut 100 kcal.
